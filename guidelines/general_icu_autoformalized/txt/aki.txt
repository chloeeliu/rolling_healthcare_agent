ICU acute kidney injury surveillance guidance.

- Monitor creatinine trajectory, urine output, KDIGO staging, and renal support.
- Stage 1 AKI is usually a suspect-level renal deterioration state.
- Stage 2 or stage 3 AKI is usually alert-level because it is more severe and actionable.
- Oliguria and severe oliguria can strengthen concern even before advanced creatinine criteria are met.
- Active CRRT indicates severe renal support.
- Search terms that may help: aki, kdigo, creatinine, urine output, crrt.
