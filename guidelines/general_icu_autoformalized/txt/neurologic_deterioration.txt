ICU neurologic surveillance guidance.

- Use GCS trend and current impairment severity as the main neurologic monitoring signal.
- Moderate impairment can remain a suspect state when the patient is stable.
- Severe impairment, especially GCS 8 or below, is usually alert-level because it is clinically actionable and often tied to airway or neurologic risk.
- Interpret neurologic data carefully in context because sedation and intubation can confound bedside scores.
- Search terms that may help: gcs, first_day_gcs, neuro.
