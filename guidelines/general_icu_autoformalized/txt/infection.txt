ICU infection surveillance guidance.

- Focus on microbiology, antibiotic exposure, cultures, fever pattern, and other evidence of active infection.
- suspected_conditions may include infection when there is meaningful concern from cultures, antibiotics, or compatible clinical evidence even if sepsis is not established.
- Stronger infection states should be separated from sepsis; infection alone does not always justify escalation.
- Search terms that may help: infection, suspicion_of_infection, antibiotic, microbiology, cultures.
