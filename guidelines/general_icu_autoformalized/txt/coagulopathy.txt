ICU coagulopathy surveillance guidance.

- Monitor INR and related coagulation abnormalities as a hematologic surveillance family.
- Mild abnormality may support suspect-level concern.
- More severe INR elevation is alert-level because it may reflect meaningful coagulopathy or bleeding risk.
- Coagulopathy should be interpreted with sepsis, liver dysfunction, shock, and procedure context when available.
- Search terms that may help: coagulation, INR, liver, sepsis.
