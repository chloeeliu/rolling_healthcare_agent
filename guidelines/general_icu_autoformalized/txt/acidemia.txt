ICU acidemia surveillance guidance.

- Monitor pH severity and its clinical context.
- Mild acidemia may support suspect-level concern when the patient is otherwise stable.
- Severe acidemia is usually alert-level because it reflects more dangerous physiologic derangement.
- Interpret pH with related signals such as lactate, ventilation, renal failure, and shock.
- Search terms that may help: bg, pH, acidemia, lactate, ventilation, aki.
