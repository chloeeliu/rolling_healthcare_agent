ICU hyperlactatemia surveillance guidance.

- Lactate is a hypoperfusion and metabolic stress marker; trend and magnitude both matter.
- Mild or moderate elevation can support suspect-level concern for shock, sepsis, or other critical illness.
- Severe hyperlactatemia is more alert-level, especially when paired with vasopressor use or acidemia.
- Lactate should often be interpreted jointly with hemodynamic and infection evidence rather than in isolation.
- Search terms that may help: bg, lactate, shock, sepsis.
