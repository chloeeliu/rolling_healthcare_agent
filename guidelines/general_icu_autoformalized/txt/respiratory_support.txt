ICU respiratory surveillance guidance.

- Monitor respiratory support intensity and gas-exchange failure separately.
- HFNC or noninvasive ventilation often represent a suspect or moderate support-escalation state.
- Invasive mechanical ventilation is usually alert-level because it reflects major support escalation.
- Severe hypoxemia, especially very low PF ratio, should increase priority and may justify escalation even if support state is unchanged.
- Search terms that may help: ventilation, invasive vent, HFNC, NIV, bg, PF ratio.
