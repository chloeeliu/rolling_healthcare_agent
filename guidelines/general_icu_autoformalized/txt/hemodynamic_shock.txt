ICU hemodynamic and shock surveillance guidance.

- Monitor vasoactive support, lactate, blood pressure, and evidence of hypoperfusion together.
- Any vasoactive support suggests important hemodynamic instability.
- Multi-agent or high-intensity vasoactive support is a stronger alert-level state.
- Shock or hypoperfusion concern rises when hypotension, vasopressors, and lactate elevation cluster together.
- Septic shock is a sepsis-linked shock state and should generally escalate.
- Search terms that may help: vasoactive_agent, norepinephrine, vasopressin, lactate, vitalsign, shock.
