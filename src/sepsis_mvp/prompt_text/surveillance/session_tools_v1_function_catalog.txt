All functions below are callable through:
{"tool_name":"call_function","arguments":{"function_name":"FUNCTION_NAME","arguments":{"stay_id":123}}}

- get_suspicion_of_infection(stay_id)
  Purpose: infection evidence timing and supporting antibiotic / culture logic for this stay.
  Use for: infection and sepsis family assessment.

- compute_sofa_score(stay_id)
  Purpose: SOFA total score plus organ-system component details.
  Use for: organ dysfunction severity, especially sepsis escalation.

- kdigo_stages(stay_id)
  Purpose: KDIGO AKI staging summary for this stay.
  Use for: AKI stage 1/2/3 reasoning.

- get_urine_output_rate(stay_id)
  Purpose: urine output rate and rolling urine-output context.
  Use for: oliguria and severe oliguria/anuria reasoning.

- ventilation_info(stay_id)
  Purpose: respiratory support and ventilation-related status for this stay.
  Use for: HFNC/NIV, invasive ventilation, respiratory support escalation.

- get_vasoactive_agent_info(stay_id)
  Purpose: vasoactive infusion exposure for this stay.
  Use for: any vasoactive support, multi-agent support, and shock-related reasoning.

- gcs(stay_id)
  Purpose: Glasgow Coma Scale observations for this stay.
  Use for: moderate or severe neurologic impairment.

- get_blood_gas_info(stay_id)
  Purpose: blood gas, lactate, pH, PF ratio, and related physiologic context.
  Use for: hypoxemia, hyperlactatemia, acidemia, and shock support.

- get_coagulation(stay_id)
  Purpose: INR and related coagulation labs for this stay.
  Use for: coagulopathy reasoning.

- get_crrt_info(stay_id)
  Purpose: CRRT support information for this stay.
  Use for: active renal replacement support.
